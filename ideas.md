# Palo Santo Bar — Digital Menu Design Brainstorm

## Three Approaches

### Approach A — "Tarifa Nights" Dark Coastal
**Theme:** Deep navy/black background with warm amber and gold accents. Evokes the Tarifa nightlife street at night — the warm glow of bar lights against the dark Andalusian sky.
**Probability:** 0.07

### Approach B — "Palo Santo Smoke & Botanicals" — CHOSEN
**Theme:** Rich dark charcoal (#1a1a1a) with warm cream/ivory text, terracotta/burnt orange accents, and botanical illustration motifs. Palo Santo wood is sacred, aromatic, and earthy — the design should feel like the wood itself: warm, natural, slightly mystical.
**Probability:** 0.08

### Approach C — "Tarifa Surf & Sun" Light Coastal
**Theme:** Bright white with ocean blue and sandy beige. Clean, fresh, Mediterranean.
**Probability:** 0.03

---

## CHOSEN APPROACH: B — "Palo Santo Smoke & Botanicals"

### Design Movement
**Dark Artisan / Botanical Luxury** — the aesthetic of a high-end mezcalería or speakeasy crossed with a Tarifa beach bar. Think: handwritten labels, dried botanicals, warm candlelight, ancient wood.

### Core Principles
1. **Warmth over coldness** — every color choice leans warm (amber, terracotta, cream, gold) against a deep charcoal base
2. **Legibility first** — menu items must be instantly scannable on a phone screen in a dark bar
3. **Botanical texture** — subtle floral/botanical motifs echo the signature floral ice cube cocktails
4. **Category clarity** — strong visual separation between sections so customers find drinks fast

### Color Philosophy
- **Background:** `#1C1A17` — deep warm charcoal (like palo santo wood at night)
- **Primary text:** `#F5EDD6` — warm ivory/cream (candlelight)
- **Accent 1:** `#C8873A` — burnt amber/terracotta (the color of palo santo smoke)
- **Accent 2:** `#8B5E3C` — dark wood brown
- **Highlight:** `#E8C97A` — warm gold (for prices and category headers)
- **Signature green:** `#4A7C59` — botanical sage (for mocktails, 0.0 section)
- **Section dividers:** subtle warm grey `#2E2A24`

### Layout Paradigm
- **Single-page scrollable menu** — no tabs, just smooth scroll with sticky category nav
- **Asymmetric card layout** — cocktails in 2-column grid, beers/drinks in compact list
- **Category anchors** — floating pill navigation at top for quick jump to section
- **Price always right-aligned** in warm gold

### Signature Elements
1. **Botanical line art dividers** — thin SVG botanical illustrations between sections
2. **Floral ice cube badge** on signature cocktails (🌸 icon with "Floral Ice Cube" label)
3. **Category color coding** — each section has a subtle left border in its accent color

### Interaction Philosophy
- Smooth scroll between sections
- Category pills highlight on scroll (intersection observer)
- Cocktail cards expand on tap to show full ingredients
- No unnecessary animations — this is a menu, not a demo

### Animation
- Page entrance: staggered fade-in of category sections (80ms delay each)
- Category pill: smooth underline slide on active state
- Card expand: 200ms ease-out height transition
- Scroll indicator: subtle fade at bottom of page

### Typography System
- **Display/Headers:** `Playfair Display` — elegant serif for category names and bar name
- **Body/Items:** `Lato` — clean, highly legible sans-serif for item names and descriptions
- **Prices:** `Lato` bold, gold color
- **Ingredients:** `Lato` light italic, muted cream

### Brand Essence
*Palo Santo is the bar where Tarifa's nightlife meets botanical craft — for the traveller who wants something more than a beer.*
Personality: **Warm. Intentional. Memorable.**

### Brand Voice
- Headlines: "Where every drink tells a story"
- CTAs: "Scan. Choose. Enjoy."
- Microcopy: "Our signature floral ice cube melts to reveal the flower inside"

### Wordmark & Logo
Stylized "PS" monogram with a botanical sprig integrated into the letterform. No default font — custom letterpress feel.

### Signature Brand Color
**Burnt Amber `#C8873A`** — the color of palo santo wood smoke.
